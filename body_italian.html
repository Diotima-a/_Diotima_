Body Parts in Italian

We often don't realize it but being able to talk about and name parts of your body in a language like Italian is surprisingly important in everyday life. To help you with that, below we have put together a list of important body parts in Italian.

English Italian Pronounciation
head la testa
arm il braccio
hand la mano
leg la gamba
knee il ginocchio
foot il piede
belly la pancia
shoulder la spalla
neck il collo
bottom il fondoschiena
back la schiena
finger il dito
toe il dito del piede
heart il cuore
nose il naso
