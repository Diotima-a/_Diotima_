Body Parts in Portuguese

We often don't realize it but being able to talk about and name parts of your body in a language like Portuguese is surprisingly important in everyday life. To help you with that, below we have put together a list of important body parts in Portuguese.

English Portuguese Pronounciation
head a cabeça
arm o braço
hand a mão
leg a perna
knee o joelho
foot o pé
belly a barriga
shoulder o ombro
neck o pescoço
bottom as nádegas
back as costas
finger o dedo
toe o dedo do pé
heart o coração
nose o nariz
