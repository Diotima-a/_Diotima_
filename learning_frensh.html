Learn Frensh

French is the language of Molière, Victor Hugo, Jean-Paul Sartre, Alain Delon, Edith Piaf, and Zinedine Zidane! Moreover, it’s the international language of fashion, cooking, theatre, visual arts, architecture, and dance. French is the fifth most widely spoken language in the world and the second most widely learned foreign language after English. It is the official language in 29 countries across different continents. Along with France, it’s widely used in Belgium, Canada, Monaco, Switzerland, Luxemburg, Chad, DR Congo, and Madagascar. About 76 million speakers consider French as their native language, and about 235 million speak it fluently. It is spoken as the first language in France, Quebec, Ontario, Belgium, Bern, Monaco, in some parts of the United States (New Hampshire, Louisiana, Vermont, and Maine), in northern Italy, and partly in Luxembourg
