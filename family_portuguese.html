Family Members in Portuguese

Want to know how to say father, mother, uncle, or aunt in Portuguese? Below we have put together a list of the most important family related words in Portuguese for you.

English Portuguese Pronounciation

husband o marido
mother a mãe
father o pai
daughter a filha
son o filho
grandfather o avô
grandfather o avô
