Learn English

Being the language of science, diplomacy, tourism, IT, the media industry, English bridges nations all over the world and contributes to progress. This is evidence of its lingua franca status. Do you want to travel anywhere in the world? English is here for you! English is one of the most widely spoken languages in the world. It is the official language in 55 countries, which include primarily the USA, the UK, Australia, and New Zealand. In 27 territories, English has the official status of the language used mainly for interactions with the government officials. This refers to Hong Kong, the Isle of Man, Puerto Rico, among many. Being a lingua franca language, it is spoken all over the world on almost all continents. It is spoken in European countries, in India, Pakistan, the Philippines, Nigeria, and others. English is the third most-spoken native language (after Chinese and Spanish) in the world. If we speak about native speakers, there are 360-400 million people worldwide. As for the number of people who learn English as a second language, this is about 750 million individuals, and 600 million learn it as a foreign language.
